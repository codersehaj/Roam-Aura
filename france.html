7-Day France Trip Plan

Explore the best of France with this amazing itinerary!

Day 1: Arrival in Paris & Eiffel Tower

Arrive in Paris and settle in your hotel. Spend your afternoon visiting the iconic Eiffel Tower and enjoy a relaxing Seine River cruise in the evening.

Day 2: Louvre Museum & Historic Paris

Explore the Louvre Museum in the morning, then stroll through historic neighborhoods like Le Marais and visit Notre-Dame Cathedral.

Day 3: Day Trip to Versailles

Take a day trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles. Explore the gardens and the opulent rooms of this royal estate.

Day 5: Bordeaux Vineyards

Day 6: French Riviera - Nice

Relax on the beaches of Nice and stroll along the Promenade des Anglais. Experience the Mediterranean charm and vibrant markets.

Day 7: Mont Saint-Michel

Visit the iconic Mont Saint-Michel, a medieval abbey perched on a rocky island. Enjoy the scenic views and charming village streets.
